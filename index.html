👤 About Dare

Dare Tunde Damilola is a Youth leader, Entrepreneur ,and change-maker who has dedicated his university years to service, impact, and progress. From volunteering at grassroots levels to representing Nigerian youth internationally, he brings a track record of proven leadership, inclusiveness, and relentless innovation.

Faculty of Law

✅ Opened Doors for Law Students

Secured 10+ internships as AGS, Oputa Chambers. Creating real legal opportunities where there were none.

✅ Redefined Excellence

As PRO, Maritime Forum became:

  • 🏅 Best Law Club in South-West Nigeria
  • 🏆 Association of the Year – Faculty of Law

✅ Connected Us to the World

Mobilized 50+ students for global programs; FUCAP (UNICEF x Unilever), Microsoft AI Skills Week, UNICEF WASH Challenge (we won), Africa University Games and more

✅ Boosted Our Academic Strength

Contributed to the review of the Oputa Note Series — simplifying difficult topics and helping more students succeed.

✅ Managed the Digital Law Student Community

Led the LSS TV team — reaching 300+ students, telling our stories, and building community beyond the classroom.

✅ Mentored the Next Generation

Inspired and guided freshers through the Miss Law Mentorship Program, turning personal struggles into shared strength.

Represented the Faculty at national platforms like UNICEF YPAT, AIESEC, Channels TV, and youth policy panels — putting Law UNILAG on the map.

✅ Bridged Law & Business

Pitched boldly at BITE 2.0, UNILAG’s top-tier business challenge, standing out among 200+ student entrepreneurs.

🎖 My brand – DXT – broke barriers as the only Faculty of Law brand in the Top 32, spotlighting our entrepreneurs and proving that law students can lead in every space.
